# London Fog
`by Conrad`

## Ingredients
1 tsp `harney & sons victorian london fog`
1 cup `water`
½ cup `milk`
 ¼ tsp `vanilla extract`
 a few `lavender flowers` for garnish

## Instructions
1. Steep tea in water for 5 minutes, then take out. 1.  Add vanilla extract to tea.
2. Microwave milk for 60 seconds, then froth until foamy.
3. Pour steamed milk on top of tea, from a large distance at first, getting closer.
4. Garnish with a few lavender flowers
